BANNER PHOTO
============

Save the UW Tacoma photo here, named exactly:

    uw-tacoma.jpg

It appears as the banner across the top of every page. Recommended size:
roughly 1600 x 600 pixels (wide and short). A .jpg works best.

To use a different filename or format, update the path in:
    _includes/header.html   (look for "uw-tacoma.jpg")

Until a photo is added, the banner shows a solid dark-purple background with
the title on top — the site still works and looks intentional.

Note on photos: use an image you have the right to publish (an official UW
Tacoma photo, a Creative Commons image, or one you took yourself). Avoid
random images found via web search, which are usually copyrighted.

You can delete this text file once the photo is in place.
