## Venue

The meeting is held at the **University of Washington Tacoma**.

- **Building / room:** JOY 117 
- **Address:** 1900 Commerce St, Tacoma, WA 98402
- **Campus map:** [tacoma.uw.edu/campus-map](https://www.tacoma.uw.edu/campus-map)

## Getting there

- **By air:** Seattle-Tacoma International Airport (SEA) is about 30–40 minutes
  by car. The bus 574 leaves from SEA and arrives at the Tacoma Dome Station, which is 1 mile from campus. Transfer to [Sound Transit Link Light Rail](https://www.soundtransit.org/system-expansion/tacoma-dome-link-extension), which takes you to the Union Station stop at UW Tacoma
- **By car:** [Hourly parking is available on campus](https://www.tacoma.uw.edu/fa/safety/transportation/hourly-parking), TPS and Tioga lots offer all-day parking for $10. 
- **By train:** Amtrak / Sounder service to Tacoma Dome Station. Transfer to [Sound Transit Link Light Rail](https://www.soundtransit.org/system-expansion/tacoma-dome-link-extension), takes you to the Union Station stop at UW Tacoma.
